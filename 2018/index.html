WHAT IS YOUR FAVORITE STORY OR MEMORY ABOUT BRIDGET?

  • I have so many! But probably practicing for Alejandro in the back of the cabin, with Bridget and Lilah, only to be interrupted because on of our campers had run away.
  • When she was little, Bridget always loved baby dolls and always had one with her. An early favorite was a baby doll made of thick rubber and then filled with what seemed like five pounds of water. Typically, she would carry the baby doll suspended by its neck in the crook of her arm. Every day of her 2-day twos preschool, Bridget would put her water baby in her Ascension Lutheran tote bag. The bag was too heavy for her to carry on her shoulder so she would literally drag the bag as we walked to school. Hers was the only bag hanging in the cubbies with drag marks burned through the canvas.

  • Most weekends from 6th-8th (maybe 9th?) grade Bridget and I would bake vanilla cakes with chocolate frosting. We would always eat it while it was still hot and the icing was dripping everywhere. Velveeta mac and cheese was usually the dinner of choice before the cake baking. Oh my god we were so healthy. Most recently, frolicking around my kitchen for hours on a cold winter night, by candle light, in nothing but our socks and underwear, while listening to Daddy Yankee.
  • So many. One of my favorites is her amazing impressions of our camp bus driver. Also her impressions of Tabitha. Also the fact that she made eveyone at cabin chat share a secret talent, then said her's was crumping and demonstrated for everyone. I just love her.

She was great back then, she's great now.

  • Just one favorite?! I have too many, but one recurring theme is that whenever I'm with Bridget, we seem to acquire other friends and comrades.
